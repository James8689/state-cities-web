Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

State Cities is a geography quiz game. This policy describes what information the app handles today. We designed the app to work without an account and without sending your gameplay to our servers.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information on our servers. The app does not require sign-in. We do not operate analytics or advertising trackers in the current version.

The app may store the following locally on your device:

  • Quiz scores and best scores (browser or app local storage)
  • Cached map files for offline play (via the app’s service worker / local storage)

This data stays on your device unless you clear app storage or uninstall the app.

Third-party data sources

Map shapes and city boundaries are bundled in the app or loaded from the app’s own static files. They are derived from public U.S. Census and Natural Earth data at build time — not fetched from third parties during normal gameplay.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences including students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes

If we add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or ads, we will update this policy before those features ship.
